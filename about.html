About Eva Hair

A calm, personal salon experience with Yulia in Gulf Harbour.

Eva Hair is a private hair salon in Gulf Harbour, owned and operated by senior stylist Yulia Kornysheva.

Established in 2010, Eva Hair offers personal hairdressing, colour, styling and makeup services in a relaxed, professional salon setting.

Yulia works one-to-one with each client to create a look that suits their hair, lifestyle and occasion. Whether you are booking a regular haircut, a fresh colour, a treatment, or wedding hair and makeup, the focus is on thoughtful service, beautiful results and a comfortable appointment experience.

Appointments are available by arrangement. Please get in touch to book a time that suits.
