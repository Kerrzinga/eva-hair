Wedding Hair & Makeup in Whangaparaoa

Bridal hair, wedding makeup, trials and bridal party styling for your day.

Feel confident, comfortable and beautifully styled on your wedding day with bridal hair and makeup by Eva Hair.

Based in Gulf Harbour, Whangaparaoa, Eva Hair offers wedding hair and makeup for brides, bridesmaids, flower girls and guests. Appointments are personal and relaxed, with styling designed to suit your dress, hair, features and the feel of your day.

Bridal Hair

Your bridal hairstyle should feel like you, only more polished. Whether you are planning soft waves, an elegant updo, curls, volume or a natural romantic style, Yulia will work with you to create a look that feels beautiful and secure for the day.

Wedding Makeup

Wedding makeup should photograph well while still feeling comfortable in person. Eva Hair offers bridal makeup, bridesmaid makeup and light makeup for flower girls, with a focus on a fresh, flattering finish.

Bridal Trials

A trial appointment is a helpful way to plan your wedding look before the day. It gives you time to discuss ideas, test the style, and make sure your hair and makeup suit your dress, accessories and overall wedding style.

Bridal Party Styling

Eva Hair can also style bridesmaids, flower girls, guests, and groom's haircuts before the wedding. If you have a bridal party, get in touch early so there is time to plan appointments and timing.
